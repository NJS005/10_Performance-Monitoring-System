:

engineering

reaching

first

:

and

problems
:

of

Modern

:
Create,

and

appropriate

engineering
and
IT

and

to

The

:

:

PO8
Ethics
:
Apply
ethical
principles
and
commit
to
professional

and

engineering

:
Function

as

and

or

in

:

with
